Hello

My name is Laurine Leung, and I am currently a web developer student at Eleven Fifty Academy. I started my career as an applications engineer in a big semiconductor corporate and I was providing design support and debug for field applications engineers from my team and customers in western European area. I travelled a lot, and I loved that!

A couple of years later and after having met my husband, I moved to the United States and pursued my career as a test engineer in a small avionics company. That's where I really learned coding (C# mostly), developing test codes, scripts, debugging, and working closely with the production team. Although there were challenging days, I really enjoyed it, and realized that it was what I wanted to do as an engineer: CODING!

Then, came the children... and my career stopped. I took time off to care for them, and during that time off, I kept learning new skills. I decided to teach French to children and to get a teaching degree. I've been teaching French for 6 years, as well as led the department chair for European languages during my last position. Through those years, I gained confidence and experience to team leading, curriculum/program development, teaching, and communicating with a diverse public.

I am now ready for a new challenge....
